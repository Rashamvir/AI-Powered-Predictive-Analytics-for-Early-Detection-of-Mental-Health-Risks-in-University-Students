## Dataset Description

**Dataset Name:** Students Mental Health Assessments  
**Source:** Kaggle  
**Link:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sonia22222/students-mental-health-assessments  

The dataset contains anonymized survey-based responses collected from university students to assess mental health conditions such as stress, anxiety, and depression.

### Data Characteristics
- Format: CSV
- Domain: Mental Health, Education
- Nature: Self-reported survey data

### Ethical Considerations
- No personally identifiable information is included
- Data is used strictly for academic and research purposes
- Predictions are not intended to replace professional medical assessment
